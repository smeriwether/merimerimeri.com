Consulting

I help teams design, build, and evaluate AI and LLM systems that are actually useful in production.

Good AI work starts with a real problem, a small loop, and a way to tell whether the system is getting better. Demos are easy. Reliable behavior is the work.

That usually means product thinking, prompt and workflow design, model integration, evaluation, and enough engineering taste to keep the feature understandable.

If that sounds like the help you need, email stephen@merimerimeri.com.

How I Help

  • Shape the idea. Pick the problem, the user path, and the smallest useful version.
  • Build the product. Integrate models, tools, data, and interfaces without making the system harder to use.
  • Evaluate the behavior. Define what good looks like, test against it, and keep improving the loop.
